 Our Story

2010

JobFinder was founded by Sahaj Brahmbhatt. A UX/UI design firm commited to helping our customers in Silicon Valley design their web & mobile experiences.

2012

We grew to about 12 team members and also offered frontend development services. We also now had clients in Gujarat.

2014

As our clients developed needs for mobile & web development, we started offering these services. Our offices expanded, about 25 team members.

2015

We made our transition from being a UX/UI/Development agency to a dedicated nearshore team model. We reinvented ourselves.

2020

Today, we work closely with our partners, continuing our mission of connecting Gujarat’s top IT talent to the world. Over 100 team members and quickly growing!
